BMI Categories

Underweight: Below 18.5 — may need healthy weight gain support.
Normal: 18.5 to 24.9 — balanced diet is recommended.
Overweight: 25 to 29.9 — weight management meals may help.
Obese: 30 or above — healthy lifestyle changes are important.
